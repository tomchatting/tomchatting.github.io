Patient 1—26 y/o male, football injury to shoulder

Partial dislocation, heals over time but would need rest. Noted how the consultant asked about thje day job to see if sick leave was needed. Offered surgery but stressed it was not aboslutely necessary. Referral in a month.

General prinicples in fractures

  1. Position
  2. Rest
  3. Rehab

A sort of fracture's food chain:

A&E → Clinic → (Surgery →) Rehab —discharge can happen at any point

Mr Parker admits that a lot of this is not exactly pharmacy oriented since they don't do much prescribing.

But: think about a staged care plan, what would most Pxs be put on in normality?

Probably NSAIDs, analgesics, aspirin, morphine(?).

Primarily, Mr Parker's work is all about keeping movement to a minimum so bones can heal naturally. Surgery considerations: success rate, scarring, complications.
